8 Game Boy songs ready to use in your GB Studio project. 
Simply put the .uge files into your project .../assets/music folder and restart GB Studio. 
All songs were made in GB Studio  4.1.3 and do also work in GBDK. 

01 Hypergolic Blast  Off is based on Creepy Organ by Beatscribe. 
02 Blue Ocean Remix is based on Blue Ocean by Coffee Bat. 
03 Darkstone  Remix  is based on Darkstone by DeerTears. 
04 Neurotic  Robonaut is a port of my own Virtual Boy music. 
05 Hideout is an original song from my discontinued Patreon project. 
06 Delight is based on the most popular western chord progression. 
07 Observing Jupiter is a tribute to the Game Boy Camera OST. 
08 Terminate is influenced by the Terminator 2 OST.
