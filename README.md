# Shift Breaker

A 2D action-platformer featuring gravity-shifting mechanics and boss-focused combat,
designed and developed in Unity.

## Overview
- Genre: 2D Action / Bullet Hell
- Platform: Mobile (Android)
- Engine: Unity
- Team Size: Solo
- Development Time: 8 months

## Role & Responsibilities
**Game Designer & Gameplay Programmer**
- Designed core gameplay systems (gravity shifting, abilities, pickups)
- Designed boss mechanics and difficulty escalation
- Implemented gameplay systems in Unity (C#)
- Designed UI and player feedback systems
- Conducted playtesting and iterative balancing

## Design Goals
- Maintain readability under high-intensity combat
- Encourage adaptive decision-making through gravity shifts
- Balance challenge and fairness
- Ensure clear UI feedback at all times

## Key Systems
**Gravity System**
- Four-direction gravity shifts with visual indicators

**Ability System**
- Temporary power-ups that create riskâ€“reward decisions

**Boss Combat**
- Escalating attack patterns with clear telegraphing

## Balancing & Iteration
Initial playtests revealed difficulty spikes caused by power stacking
and pickup frequency. Through iteration, cooldowns, fire rates, and
spawn timings were adjusted to improve pacing and fairness.
